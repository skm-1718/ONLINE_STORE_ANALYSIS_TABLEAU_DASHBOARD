# 📊 Online Retail Store Analysis | Tableau Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This Tableau project analyzes an online retail dataset to provide actionable business insights for executive decision-making. The dashboard answers strategic business questions for the CEO and CMO by visualizing revenue trends, customer performance, geographic demand, and sales distribution.

The project demonstrates how data visualization can transform raw transactional data into meaningful insights that support business growth and operational efficiency.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

Develop an executive dashboard that enables leadership to:

- Monitor overall business performance
- Identify revenue growth opportunities
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior
- Compare country-wise sales performance
- Support strategic business decisions using data

## 📂 Dataset

**Dataset:** Online Retail Dataset

### Data Cleaning Performed
- Removed records with Quantity ≤ 1
- Removed Unit Price ≤ 0
- Removed negative transactions
- Standardized and validated data before analysis

## 📈 Dashboard Components

### Q1: Monthly Revenue Trend (CEO)

- Analyzed monthly revenue throughout 2011
- Revenue showed steady growth during the year
- Peak revenue recorded in October and November
- Slight decline observed in December

---

### Q2: Top 10 Countries by Revenue (Excluding UK)

- Compared revenue generated by top international markets
- Netherlands generated the highest revenue
- Ireland and Germany followed closely
- Quantity sold was also compared across countries

---

### Q3: Top 10 Customers by Revenue

- Identified highest-value customers
- Ranked customers based on total revenue
- Helps improve customer retention strategies
- Supports targeted marketing initiatives

---

### Q4: Country-wise Demand Analysis

- Interactive world map showing quantity sold
- Darker regions indicate higher demand
- Highlights potential markets for business expansion

---

## 📌 Key Business Insights

- Revenue increased consistently throughout 2011.
- October and November recorded the highest sales.
- Netherlands is the strongest international market.
- A small group of customers contributes significantly to total revenue.
- Demand is concentrated in selected European countries, indicating opportunities for targeted expansion.
